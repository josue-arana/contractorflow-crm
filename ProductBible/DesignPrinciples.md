# Design Principles

- Simple beats clever. The product should feel easy on first use.
- Mobile-first by default. Core flows must work smoothly on a phone before desktop refinement.
- No enterprise jargon. Use plain contractor language in English and Spanish.
- Every button works. Avoid placeholders, dead ends, and fake controls in visible UI.
- Reuse patterns. Familiar layouts, actions, modals, and feedback should stay consistent across the app.
